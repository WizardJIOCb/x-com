Do not export the raw HD mesh directly if it has hundreds of thousands or millions of faces.

Recommended Tripo flow:

1. Open one character or monster folder.
2. Upload upload.png in Image-to-3D.
3. Paste prompt.txt as the text prompt.
4. Generate 3 to 6 variants and keep the cleanest full-body silhouette.
5. Reject models with fused arms, fused legs, fused weapons, missing fingers, or strange flat backs.
6. Run Smart Mesh / Retopology before export.
7. Target mesh budget:
   - normal mobs: 10k to 25k triangles
   - heroes: 30k to 60k triangles
   - bosses: 50k to 100k triangles
8. Run Auto Rig from the Animation section.
9. Test idle, walk, run, attack-like motion if Tripo offers previews.
10. Export as FBX with mesh, materials, textures, skeleton, and animations enabled.
11. Send to Unreal through DCC Bridge or download the FBX and import manually.

Unreal import target:

/Game/Characters/Tripo/<asset_id>/

For final game characters, keep guns and melee weapons as separate Unreal meshes. Do not accept a hero model where a weapon is permanently fused into the hand unless it is only a temporary prototype.
