Best export settings for Crimson Wars:

Format: FBX
Use case: Unreal Engine skeletal mesh
Include: textures, materials, skeleton, skin weights
Texture size: 2048 for normal mobs, 4096 for heroes and bosses
Pose before rig: T-pose or A-pose
Topology: Smart Mesh / retopologized, not the raw HD triangle mesh
Scale: human hero around 180 cm, normal infected around 170-190 cm, bosses 260-380 cm

After import in Unreal:

1. Check the skeleton in Persona.
2. Play an idle/walk animation.
3. Check shoulders, elbows, hips, knees, neck, and jaw if present.
4. Create sockets for weapons instead of using fused weapons.
5. Keep source FBX and textures in the same folder as the imported asset.
